Privacy Policy

WW Badges — Win-Win Apps  |  Last updated: April 3, 2026

Win-Win Apps ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WW Badges Shopify application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you install WW Badges, we collect and store the following data to operate the app:

  • Shopify store URL — to identify your store and associate your badge configurations
  • Shopify access token — to sync badge data to your store's metafields and read product/theme information
  • Badge configurations — the badge designs, targeting rules, and settings you create within the app

We do not collect or store any personal information about your customers (shoppers).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your configured badges on your storefront via Shopify metafields and theme app extensions
  • To store your badge settings so they persist between sessions
  • To authenticate API requests to Shopify on your behalf

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

3. Data Storage

Badge configurations and session data are stored in a secure database hosted on our production servers. Access tokens are encrypted at rest. We retain your data for the duration of your app installation.

4. Third-Party Services

WW Badges uses the following third-party services:

  • Shopify API — to read product data, set metafields, and manage theme extensions on your store. Shopify's privacy policy is available at shopify.com/legal/privacy.
  • Fly.io — our application hosting provider. Data is processed in accordance with Fly.io's data processing terms.

5. Data Deletion

When you uninstall WW Badges, we receive a mandatory app/uninstalled webhook from Shopify. Upon receiving this webhook, we permanently delete all data associated with your store, including badge configurations and access tokens.

To request manual data deletion, contact us at omar@wwapps.io.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data storage, HTTPS-only communication, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
